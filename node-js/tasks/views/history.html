History of lorem ipsum text

Lorem Ipsum comes from passages of a latin book written in 45 BC by Cicero "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" translated to "The Extremes of Good and Evil".

As you can see, although, you may not have the content ready for your site or whatever it is that you want to publish you can get a feel for what it would look like. I hope Cicero is happy to know that we felt his text was so good that we now use it to fill up bank pages everywhere, or maybe that was just what he was going for? If so, kudos to you Cicero.
